CRACKIT!

Our project, CRACKIT!, extracts commentary ball-by-ball and uses Machine Learning to judge whether any delivery is capable of changing the outcome of the match. Then, it displays the highlights on our web page. The user has to choose the match of his choice from a set of recent matches and that's it! The main motivation behind choosing this theme has been the passion of masses towards the sport and important moments of the match can serve as a way to experience the entire match in a matter of minutes.
